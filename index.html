Projects

LGBTQ Data Analysis based on Quotebank

This project aims to analyze the quotation dataset Quotebank and uncover hidden information related to LGBTQ topics. It starts by using BERT language models to recognize LGBTQ topics and derives various data distribution statistics on LGBTQ. The project further explores the sentimental factor of the quotations related to LGBTQ and conducts correlation analysis on education and income levels regarding the acceptance of LGBTQ.
